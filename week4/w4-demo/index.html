ABOUT Deadpool & Wolverine Movie

Deadpool and Wolverine are two fictional superheroes created by the Marvel Comics team. They are known for their iconic costumes and unique abilities.

Deadpool: A former mercenary turned superhero, Deadpool uses his skills to fight crime and protect the world.

Wolverine: A powerful and fearless mutant with extraordinary strength and agility, Wolverine is known for his adamantium claws and accelerated healing factor.

Genre: Action, Comedy, Bromance.

Director: Shawn Levy

released date: 2024

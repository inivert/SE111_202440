Constantine is a supernatural thriller that follows John Constantine, a cynical occult detective who can communicate with half-angels and half-demons in their true form. Struggling with his faith and smoking habit, Constantine seeks to earn his way back into God's favor by sending demons back to hell.

Company Credits: Warner Bros. Pictures

Release Date: February 18, 2005

Genres: Action, Fantasy, Horror

Rating: R

Running time: 121 minutes
